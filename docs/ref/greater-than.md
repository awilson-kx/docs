# `>` Greater Than <br>`>=` At Least




Syntax: `x > y` , `>[x;y]`  
Syntax: `x >= y`, `>=[x;y]`

Returns `1b` where (items of) `x` are greater than (or at least) `y`.

```q
q)(3;"a")>(2 3 4;"abc")
100b
000b
q)(3;"a")>=(2 3 4;"abc")
110b
100b
```

Greater Than and At Least are atomic functions.

With booleans:

```q
q)0 1 >/:\: 0 1
00b
10b
q)0 1 >=/:\: 0 1
10b
11b
```
